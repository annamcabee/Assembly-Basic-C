Instructions:
To win the game, you must navigate the pug to the cookies.
You must avoid the enemies (Ryan Gosling's) or you will die.
You have 3 lives.

Controls:
In the main part of the game:
	Arrow Right to move the pug to the right
	Arrow Left to move the pug to the left
	Arrow Up to the move the pug upwards
	Arrow Down to move the pug downwards
At any time:
	Select Button to go to title screen
